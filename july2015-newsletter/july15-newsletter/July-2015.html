The "Charles Sawtelle Memorial" Mountain Jam


Sunday, July 19, 2015 11am-4:30pm
The Gold Hill Inn, Gold Hill, CO

Celebrate 27 years of the Mountain Jam in July. KGNU's Annual 'Charles' Mountain Jam will be held Sunday, July 19, in the side yard of the Gold Hill Inn in Gold Hill, Colorado. A KGNU tradition, the "Charles" is named in honor of KGNU supporter and Hot Rize guitarist Charles Sawtelle who helped start the event in 1989.

This is a family-friendly event for music fans that enjoy great music and delicious food in a spectacular setting. The gate opens at 11:00 a.m. and the music runs 11:30am -4:30 pm, featuring sets by Chain Station, Sierra Hull, and Frank Solivan & Dirty Kitchen. Tickets are $20 for KGNU members, $25 for the general public, and are available now online through Brown Paper Tickets; more details can also be found through our website.
