Latest Updates & Station News

The 'Charles Sawtelle Memorial' Mountain Jam


Sunday, July 19, 2015 11am-4:30pm
The Gold Hill Inn, Gold Hill, CO

Celebrate 27 years of the Mountain Jam in July. KGNU's Annual 'Charles' Mountain Jam will be held Sunday, July 19, in the side yard of the Gold Hill Inn in Gold Hill, Colorado. A KGNU tradition, the “Charles” is named for KGNU supporter and Hot Rize guitarist Charles Sawtelle who helped start the event in 1989.

This is a family-friendly event for music fans that enjoy great music and delicious food in a spectacular setting. The gate opens at 11:00 a.m. and the music runs 11:30 a.m.-4:30 pm, featuring sets by Chain Station, Sierra Hull, and Frank Solivan & Dirty Kitchen. Tickets are $20 for KGNU members, $25 for the general public, and are available now online through Brown Paper Tickets; more details can also be found through our website.

Summer Mini-Membership Drive: July 7th to July 12th


Help us raise $30,000 for our summer mini-membership drive. We need to raise this money in order to meet our budget for 2015. Most of the money it takes to bring you the critical news and diverse music you depend on comes direct from individual supporters. We are so grateful to each and every person who makes KGNU possible. Please keep the music, news, and community voices flowing by becoming a new or renewing member or make an additional contribution today by pledging online or by calling us at 303-449-4885. Thanks!

When you donate now, we’ll automatically enter you into several drawings we’ll be doing during the summer drive, including drawings for a great Colorado rafting trip, a Grand Lake Getaway, and a night on the town in Denver with accommodation at The Curtis. More details will be posted on our website later this month.

As always, KGNU relies on volunteer power to answer phones and take pledges during our membership drives. You can sign up now online to help answer phones. No experience necessary.

Summer Record Sale


KGNU Community Radio is putting out the vinyl call for our Summer Record Sale parking lot party on Sunday June 28th at our Denver home, 700 Kalamath St. Going digital with your music collection? More details may be found on our website.


Summer Youth Radio Training


Do you want to get radio-active? KGNU is holding our summer radio training workshop for ages 13-18, July 20, 21 and 22, 9am-3pm. Participants will learn how to be a music DJ and how to produce a news story for radio. The cost is $150 for the 3 day workshop. Enrollment is limited, so email or call Nikki (nikki@kgnu.org, 303-449-4885) to reserve your place!
